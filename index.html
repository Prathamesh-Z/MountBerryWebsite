Our Story

Authentic Coffee.
Honest Roasts.

At Mount Berry Coffee Roasters, we carefully select only the finest specialty green coffee beans from the high altitudes of Chikmagalur, Karnataka — known for its density and exceptional flavour profile.

Once sourced, we roast them right here in Karad using expert techniques to bring out their full potential. Our roasted beans are then distributed across India — letting coffee lovers experience the unique flavours of high-quality Arabica in every cup.
